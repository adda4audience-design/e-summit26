Base Camp (Accommodation)

Secure your space inside the UIET campus. Accommodations include 24/7 high-speed Wi-Fi, 3 daily meals in the mess, and round-the-clock security. Note: Allotment is strictly based on successful registration for non-UIET delegates.

How to Reach

By Bus (From Delhi/Chandigarh)

Take any bus traveling on NH-44 (GT Road) and drop off at Pipli Bus Stand. From Pipli, you can take a local auto-rickshaw directly to Kurukshetra University (3rd Gate is closest to UIET).

By Train

Book your tickets to Kurukshetra Junction (KKDE). The station is well connected to major hubs like New Delhi and Ambala. From the station, shared autos and cabs are readily available to the university campus (approx. 15 mins).

Inside Campus

Enter through Gate No. 3 of Kurukshetra University. UIET is located in the southern sector of the university. Follow the directional signage for "E-Summit".
