Transit Protocols

Select your transport vector below to reach the coordinates.

Bus

Drop off at Pipli Bus Stand via NH-44 (GT Road). Auto-rickshaws run directly from the stand to Kurukshetra University Gate 3.

Train

Arrive at Kurukshetra Junction (KKDE). The station provides shared autos and cabs directly to the campus (approx. 15 min ride).

Internal Nav

Enter through Gate No. 3 of the university. Head toward the southern sector following E-Summit event signage directly to UIET.
