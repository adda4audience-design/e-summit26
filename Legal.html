Privacy Policy

How we handle the data you hand over to the system

01 What We Collect

When you register for E-Summit 2026, we collect information you provide directly: your name, email address, phone number, college/institution details, and event preferences. If you participate in paid events, payment-related data is processed through our payment partner and is not stored on our servers.

02 Why We Collect It

  • To process your registration and confirm your spot at events
  • To send updates, schedules, and entry passes via email or SMS
  • To coordinate logistics such as accommodation and team formation
  • To improve future editions of E-Summit based on participation trends

03 Who Sees It

Your data is accessible only to the E-Summit organizing team and our verified sponsors for event-relevant communication, where applicable. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

04 Data Retention

We retain registration data for the duration of the event cycle and a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping and future outreach. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

05 Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by writing to esummit@uietkuk.ac.in. We aim to respond to all such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Note: This policy applies only to data collected through the official E-Summit 2026 website and registration channels.

Terms & Conditions

The rules of engagement for entering the ecosystem

01 Eligibility

E-Summit 2026 is open to students, professionals, and entrepreneurs as specified per event. Some events may have specific eligibility criteria (team size, academic year, institution type) which will be listed on individual event pages.

02 Registration

  • All information provided during registration must be accurate and complete
  • Registrations are confirmed only after successful payment, where applicable
  • One registration per participant per event unless otherwise stated
  • The organizing team reserves the right to reject or cancel any registration that appears fraudulent or duplicated

03 Code of Conduct

Participants are expected to maintain respectful conduct throughout the summit. Harassment, plagiarism, or disruptive behavior at any event may result in disqualification or removal from the premises without refund.

04 Event Changes

The organizing committee reserves the right to modify event schedules, venues, speakers, or formats due to unforeseen circumstances. Participants will be notified of significant changes via the contact details provided at registration.

05 Intellectual Property

Any work, pitch, or project submitted during hackathons or competitions remains the intellectual property of the participant(s) unless otherwise agreed in writing with sponsors or partners involved in that specific event.

06 Limitation of Liability

UIET KUK and the E-Summit organizing team are not liable for personal injury, loss of property, or damages incurred during travel to, from, or within the event premises, except where caused by proven organizational negligence.

Refund Policy

Once the payment clears, the slot is locked

All payments made for E-Summit 2026 are final and non-refundable. Once a registration fee is successfully paid, it cannot be cancelled, reversed, or refunded under any circumstance, except for the limited technical-error cases listed in Section 03 below.

01 General Policy

Registration fees go directly toward securing venues, kits, vendor bookings, and logistics well in advance of the event. Because these commitments are made on your behalf as soon as payment is confirmed, we do not offer refunds for change of mind, personal unavailability, withdrawal, or any other reason once a transaction is successful.

02 No-Refund Scenarios

To be clear, the following situations are not eligible for any refund, partial or full:

  • Change of mind or voluntary withdrawal after payment
  • Participant no-show on the day of the event
  • Inability to attend due to personal, academic, or travel reasons
  • Disqualification due to a code of conduct violation
  • Incorrect event/track selected by the participant at the time of registration
  • Dissatisfaction with event content, speakers, or schedule changes

03 The Only Exceptions

A refund will be considered only in the following technical-error cases, and nowhere else:

  • A duplicate payment was charged for the same registration due to a platform or gateway error
  • Payment was deducted from your account but no registration was ever created (i.e. the transaction failed on our end)
  • The event is cancelled in its entirety by the organizing committee

04 Reporting an Exception

  • 1 Email us at esummit@uietkuk.ac.in within 7 days of the transaction, with subject line "Payment Error – [Your Registration ID]".
  • 2 Attach proof: payment confirmation/transaction ID, and a screenshot of the bank statement showing the deduction, if available.
  • 3 Verification: our team checks the transaction against our records within 3–5 business days to confirm whether it qualifies under Section 03.
  • 4 If approved, the refund is processed back to your original payment method within 10–15 business days. If not approved, no refund will be issued and the team's decision is final.

05 Before You Pay

Since payments are final, please double-check the event, track, date, and team details before completing your registration. If you're unsure about anything, reach out to us first at esummit@uietkuk.ac.in — it's easier to answer a question than to process a non-refundable mistake.

Cookie Policy

The small trackers running quietly in the background

01 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help our website function properly and remember your preferences, such as your login session on the dashboard.

02 Types We Use

  • Essential cookies — required for login sessions and dashboard access
  • Performance cookies — help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it
  • Preference cookies — remember settings like menu state across visits

03 Third-Party Cookies

Some embedded content, such as fonts and icon libraries, may load resources from third-party CDNs that set their own minimal cookies. We do not control these directly.

04 Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Note that disabling essential cookies may prevent dashboard login and certain site features from working correctly.
